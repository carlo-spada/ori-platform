# Ori Brand Essentials

## Core Identity

**Brand Promise:** "Your career, continuously aligned."

**Positioning:** Autonomous AI career companion that guides professionals to fulfilling work through personalized insights and continuous support.

**Personality:** Serene, Whimsical, Intuitive, Empathetic

---

## Visual Identity

### Mascot: Ori the Firefly

- **Concept:** Futuristic firefly-inspired AI guide (EVE-like smooth design + bioluminescent glow)
- **Silhouette:** Rounded orb/capsule with minimal details
- **Colors:** Pearlescent white/silver body with blue/amber glow
- **Expression:** Minimal digital eyes (kind, calm by default)
- **Usage:** Empty states, onboarding, celebrations, loading indicators

### Color Palette

**Dark Mode (Primary):**

- Background: `#0D1B2A` (deep navy)
- Panels: `#1B2635` (lighter dark)
- Primary Accent: `#3EDBF0` (electric blue)
- Warm Accent: `#F9A72B` (amber)
- Text: `#E0E0E0` (light gray), `#FFFFFF` (headings)
- Success: `#3EE8A0` (teal-green)
- Error: `#FF5E5E` (soft red)

**Light Mode (Secondary):**

- Background: `#F5F7FA` (off-white)
- Cards: `#FFFFFF`
- Primary: Darker blue variants for contrast
- Text: `#2A2A2A` (charcoal)

### Typography

- **Headings:** Geometric sans (Circular/Google Sans) - Medium to Bold
- **Body:** Humanist sans (Inter/Roboto) - Regular
- **Scale:** 16px base, generous line-height (1.6)
- **Case:** Sentence case preferred (avoid all-caps)

## UI/UX Principles

### Layout

- **Spacious:** Generous padding (16-24px base grid)
- **Clean:** Card-based surfaces, minimal borders
- **Rounded:** 6-8px corner radius on all components
- **Layered:** Subtle shadows (light) or glows (dark) for depth

### Components

- **Buttons:** Pill-shaped or 6px radius, primary blue with white text
- **Cards:** Rounded corners, subtle elevation, 16px padding minimum
- **Inputs:** Minimal borders, blue accent on focus
- **Icons:** Rounded line icons (1.5px stroke), 24px size

### Motion

- **Gentle:** Slow easing transitions (300-500ms)
- **Meaningful:** Animations celebrate or acknowledge (not decorative)
- **Subtle:** Ori mascot blinks/glows occasionally (not constantly)
- **Respectful:** Honor `prefers-reduced-motion`
